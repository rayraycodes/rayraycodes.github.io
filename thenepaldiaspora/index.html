The Crisis: Why We Must Convene Now

Nepal's greatest asset—its people—is at a crossroads. Global forces demand immediate strategic action.

The Human Capital Exodus

Over a million bright, ambitious and hardworking citizens depart Nepal annually. This "brain drain" is a failure of domestic strategic investment that threatens long-term autonomy and productivity.

The AI Disruption Risk

Skills learned through rote instruction are rapidly becoming obsolete. Nepal's workforce is unprepared for the AI-augmented global economy, necessitating a massive, proactive reskilling effort.

Geopolitical & Policy Vacuum

A changing world order requires sophisticated, ethical leadership. Without strategic policy, Nepal risks losing its competitive edge and decision-making power in the South Asian century.
